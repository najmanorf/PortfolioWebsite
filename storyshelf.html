Project Overview

StoryShelf is a mobile app concept designed to help users create aesthetic movie and book reviews effortlessly while organizing key insights and quotes in a structured way. Acting as Product Strategist and UI/UX Designer, I defined the product direction based on user research that revealed frustration with time-consuming manual editing, unorganized notes, and difficulty deciding what to watch or read next.

I led end-to-end product strategy and design, from research and problem framing to user flows and high-fidelity UI. Insights from surveys, interviews, and competitive analysis guided feature prioritization, interaction design, and usability improvements. The final solution balances aesthetic expression with functional clarity, enabling users to document, revisit, and share their experiences seamlessly.

Key Features

Aesthetic Review Templates

Ready-to-use and customizable templates that help users create visually appealing movie or book reviews without manual layout editing, reducing effort while maintaining a personal creative touch.

Insight & Quote Organization

A structured note system that allows users to save key insights and favorite quotes with tags and contextual search, making information easy to retrieve without endless scrolling.

Random Picker & Recommendations

A discovery feature that suggests movies or books through a random picker and refreshed recommendations, helping users decide what to watch or read next with less decision fatigue.

Reading & Watching Statistics

A visual recap that shows users their watching and reading history, helping them track habits, reflect on preferences, and gain meaningful insights from their activity over time.
