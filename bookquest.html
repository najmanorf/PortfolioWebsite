BookQuest

Gamified Reading & Quiz

  • Role : Product & UI/UX Designer
  • Team : 3 Members
  • Timeline : May 2026
  • Try BookQuest Prototype Here

Project Overview

Problem

Digital reading often feels passive and monotonous, causing users to lose motivation and struggle to retain key insights after reading.

Target Users

High school and college students, book readers, self-improvement enthusiasts, and lifelong learners.

Solution

A digital library app that combines gamified reading, chapter quizzes, daily quests, and affordable short-term book lending.

Product Goal

Make reading more engaging, affordable, and effective by helping users build consistent habits and understand what they read.

Feature Showcase

Each feature was designed based on a specific user pain point identified during product discovery.

01

Gamified Reading Experience

Problem

Readers often lose motivation because digital reading can feel passive, repetitive, and less rewarding than other digital entertainment.

Feature Solution

Users earn XP, level up, complete daily reading quests, and collect rewards such as gems, making reading feel more interactive and motivating.

02

Quiz for Every Chapter

Problem

Users often finish reading without fully understanding or remembering the key insights from each chapter.

Feature Solution

Each chapter includes interactive quizzes that help users check comprehension, retain important ideas, and stay actively engaged with the book.

03

Affordable 7-Day Book Lending

Problem

Many students face limited access to books because permanent access or purchasing books can be financially difficult.

Feature Solution

Users can borrow digital book access for affordable short-term periods starting from 7 days, making books easier to access without full purchase costs.
