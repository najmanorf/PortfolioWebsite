Project Overview

Problem

The Borma Dago app had usability gaps in navigation, system feedback, and information hierarchy, making the experience less intuitive.

Target Users

Borma Dago customers who use the mobile app to browse products, check promotions, and make shopping decisions more efficiently.

Solution

A revamp based on heuristic evaluation, focusing on clearer navigation, stronger system feedback, and improved hierarchy.

Product Goal

Improve usability, shopping confidence, and product discovery by aligning the interface with user behavior and retail business needs.

Feature Showcase

Each feature was designed based on a specific user pain point identified during product discovery.

01

Category-Based Product Navigation

Problem

Users may feel confused when browsing daily necessities if product categories are unclear or inconsistent.

Feature Solution

Products are organized into clear and consistent categories to help users browse items more intuitively. This improvement aligns with users’ mental models in retail shopping and reduces confusion when searching for daily necessities.

02

Clear Promotion & Price Visibility

Problem

Promotions, discounts, and price information are displayed more prominently and consistently, ensuring users immediately understand ongoing offers without needing extra steps or assumptions.

Feature Solution

Promotions, discounts, and price information are displayed more clearly so users can immediately understand ongoing offers.

03

Enhanced Cart Feedback

Problem

Users may feel uncertain whether their add-to-cart or update actions have been successfully completed.

Feature Solution

Users receive clear visual feedback when adding or updating items in the cart, improving system visibility and reducing uncertainty about whether actions have been successfully completed.

04

Improved Product Information Hierarchy

Problem

Users need quick access to key product details such as price, stock, and promotions before deciding what to buy.

Feature Solution

Product details such as price, stock availability, and promotions are structured in a clearer hierarchy, enabling users to compare items quickly and make confident purchase decisions.
