BundaSehat

Daily Pregnancy Checkup

2nd Place HackHERthon 2025

  • Role : Product & UI/UX Designer
  • Team : 2 Members
  • Date : December 2025
  • Prototype : bit.ly/BundaSehatMVP

Project Overview

Problem

Indonesia's Maternal Mortality Rate remains 189 deaths per 100,000 live births, far above the UN global target of 70.

Target Users

Expectant mothers, especially those in underserved and rural communities with limited access to maternal healthcare.

Solution

A digital maternal health platform with daily checkups, localized health education, and personalized recommendations.

Product Goal

Reduce preventable pregnancy risks through accessible, proactive, and personalized maternal healthcare support.

Feature Showcase

Each feature was designed based on a specific user pain point identified during product discovery.

01

Pregnancy Daily Check-Up

Problem

Pregnant women often struggle to monitor their health consistently, making it difficult to recognize early warning signs.

Feature Solution

A self-monitoring tool for daily tracking of nutrition, energy, mood, and symptoms, designed to detect potential health risks early and guide users toward proactive pregnancy care.

02

Mind-Map Library

Problem

Pregnancy information is often scattered, difficult to understand, and inaccessible for first-time mothers.

Feature Solution

An interactive visual library that transforms complex medical information into easy-to-understand mind maps covering nutrition, herbal remedies, fetal development, and pregnancy danger signs.

03

Personalized Health Insights

Problem

Generic pregnancy advice often fails to address each mother's unique health condition and daily needs.

Feature Solution

A recommendation engine that delivers personalized health guidance and locally relevant meal suggestions based on each user's daily pregnancy check-up results.

04

Health Scoring & Flagging

Problem

Many expectant mothers cannot easily determine whether their symptoms require immediate medical attention.

Feature Solution

A color-coded health scoring system that converts daily check-up results into Safe, Caution, and Danger levels, helping users identify risks early and take timely action.
